YOUR SAFETY

Safety

ITC Logistics is a member of the partners in Protection Program (PIP). This program is designed to enhance border security, combat organized crime and terrorism, increase awareness of customs compliance issues, and help detect and prevent contraband smuggling. Being a part of a safety program such as PIP holds ITC Logistics accountable, and ensures the safety of your products as they move. We are focused on providing the safest and most secure avenue of shipping for our customers. These safety measures eliminate any risk factor that may come with transporting goods. Safety and security for our customer’s freight is our first priority, and we will continue to ensure that our safety policy is upheld to the highest standard.
